About Me & More

Hello, my name is Wei-Jie Gao. I am currently a Senior and finishing the rest of my degree for Computer Science at Texas A&M University. My goal as of Fall 2022 is to complete my degree and find a Software Engineer Job Position for after my graduation. This website is a project that teaches us on HTML basics and web-design from my ISTM 209 course. I personally have done multiple project similar to this, and suprisingly, I still enjoy the process of creating a site from scratch. If you would like to view my other works, visit my Github! Also, if you would like to connect with me this is my LinkedIn!

Lastly, just some reflection over my time here at A&M. I had many close calls with several courses, but I work through each one of them. My favorite course is CSCE 431 or Software Engineering, because the class was part of a study abroad program. We had a chance to visit Greece and learn basic Agile Programming Process throughout the wintermester. If given the chance to do it again, I would sign-up in a heart-beat!
